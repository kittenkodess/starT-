Federal Recognition

In December 2024, starT had the distinct honor of receiving a Special Recognition Award from the United States House of Representatives. This prestigious acknowledgment was granted as part of the Congressional App Challenge, recognizing starT's innovative first concept design and its potential to create real impact.

Our Team

We are an all-girls team, founded in the vibrant city of Chicago by passionate POC teens who experienced firsthand the challenges and inequities in the college application process. Recognizing a significant gap in accessibility and fairness, we created StarT to modernize opportunities for all students. Our team is composed of talented, ambitious young women—primarily women of color—dedicated to continually improving StarT and expanding its impact across the nation and, ultimately, the globe.
